Mural Nomad

Mural Nomad is a project where I explore our relationship with technology using a blend of hand & digitally made images, symbols and non linear storytelling to create abstract moments in time. Inspired by art history, technology, cartoons, music and new art processes.
I developed the Shopify web store & designed the logo for this site, where you can find and purchase my tapestries and drawing work.

Change My View

A personal favorite, Change My View Reader was built based on the popular SubReddit page "Change My View" where we built a Mobile-First reader of views and opinions using RESTful API's to fetch the information which we organized and displayed in a fresh way to show the different sides of the original project. User is able to search and browse by topic. Branding and design by me, front end by my partner and I.

Goteya Logo

Goteya is a brand based in Brooklyn, New York, and is owned and operated by women and immigrants.
Their aim is "to provide fashion-forward and accessible eye-wear to people around the world." Goteya asked me to create a new logo for the online store and I was inspired by their focus on accessibility and affordability. Goteya borrows its name from Joteya, the off-brand section of a Moroccan souk which is where one of their founders was born.
